About Us

Welcome to Racer, the first-ever Algerian race-hosting platform! We are proud to bring a diverse range of competitive events to athletes and enthusiasts across the nation. Whether you are passionate about marathons, triathlons, rallies, swimming, cycling, or motorcycling races — we have something for everyone.

At Racer, our mission is to promote the spirit of competition and athleticism in Algeria. We believe in providing top-notch race experiences, ensuring every participant enjoys a seamless and exhilarating event. Our team is dedicated to organizing and hosting high-quality races that challenge and inspire athletes of all levels.

Join us on this exciting journey as we pave the way for a vibrant racing community in Algeria. Lace up your running shoes, tune up your bike, or rev your engine — let's race together!
